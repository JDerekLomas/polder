# MakeMode — AI Transparency Notice

**Version:** v0 draft · **Last updated:** [DATE]

## You are using an AI system

MakeMode includes an **artificial-intelligence assistant**. When you use it, **you are interacting with an automated AI system, not a human.** The code, text, and suggestions it gives you are **generated by AI.**

## Please review AI-generated output

AI output can be **inaccurate, incomplete, insecure, or unsuitable** for your purpose, and may resemble existing material. **Always review, test, and verify anything the assistant produces before you rely on it, publish it, or submit it** — especially for coursework, where your institution's academic-integrity rules apply.

## What happens to what you type

When you prompt the assistant, your prompt and the relevant parts of your code and project are **sent to an EU-hosted AI model provider** so a response can be generated. Avoid entering personal or confidential data you are not authorised to share. See the **Privacy Policy** for the full data flow.

## Where MakeMode sits under the EU AI Act

MakeMode is a tool for **building and prototyping** and is treated as a **limited-risk AI system** under the EU AI Act. Our obligation at this level is **transparency** — making clear to you that you are interacting with AI and that output is AI-generated, which is the purpose of this notice. MakeMode is **not** intended for, and must **not** be used for, **student assessment, grading, or other high-risk decision-making** about people.
